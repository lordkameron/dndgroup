Episode 1: The Church With A View

×

A church fueled on hatred becomes the beacon for ghosts and poltergeists to cross over to our world. They possess the stained glass windows and kill all who dare cross them.

Episode 2 & 3: The Plague of Webs

Original Airdate: N/A

Doctor Atsuko Popov is a scientist-turned spider-person from a Cold War weapons test gone wrong. Now in modern times she continues to test the bioweapon that created her, inadvertantly creating a plague that stands to mutate the world into spiders.

Episode 4: The Circles

Original Airdate: 2020-12-14

An immortal Minotaur, Pavlos, needs to feed upon human flesh every 7 years to maintain his life. He does this by traveling across the US disguised as a farmer, and lures folks into his cornfield advertising "Alien sightings". He needs one more meal before he moves out and our intrepid few are next on the menu!

Episode 5: The Attack of the Rapid Moss

Original Airdate: 2020-12-21

Two scientists in a remote forest laboratory engineer an intelligent "Learning Moss". But the Moss yearns for world domination. By the time our heroes have already stumbled upon the mossy knolls, it may already be too late!
